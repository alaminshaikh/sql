Class 9 IT Communication Skills

Q1. What is Communication and why is it important?

Ans: The act of giving, receiving, and sharing information is known as communication. Signs and signals provide information. Communication can take the form of speaking, writing, or any other medium. Communication helps us to build good relationships between friends and families.

Q2. Write all the elements of Communication Cycle.

Ans: Elements of communication cycle are:

  1. Sender: the person beginning the communication.
  2. Message: the information that the sender wants to convey.
  3. Channel: the means by which the information is sent.
  4. Receiver: the person to whom the message is sent.
  5. Feedback: the receiver’s acknowledgement and response to the message.

Q3. What do you mean by perspective in communication? Explain with example.

Ans: Perspectives are ideas, views, or fixed ways of thinking. For example, if you have a fixed idea that your teacher or father is strict, even when they are being friendly, you may think they are scolding you even though they are polite.

Q4. Name three different methods of communication.

Ans: Three different methods of communication are:

  1. Verbal Communication
  2. Non-Verbal Communication
  3. Visual Communication

Q5. What do you mean by verbal communication?

Ans: Verbal communication is the sharing of information by using words. It is what most people use as a method of communication.

Q6. Mention the advantages of Verbal Communication.

Ans: Advantages of verbal communication are:

  1. It is an easy mode of communication.
  2. In verbal communication we get a quick response.
  3. Verbal communication also enables us to keep changing our interaction as per the other person’s response.

Q7. Write the disadvantages of Verbal Communication.

Ans: Verbal communication depends on written or spoken words, sometimes the meanings can be confusing and difficult to understand if the right words are not used.

Q8. What do you mean by non-verbal communication?

Ans: When we send signals or messages to others, through expressions, gestures and body postures. This is called non-verbal communication.

Q9. Name any four ways of non-verbal communication.

Ans: Four ways of non-verbal communication are:

  • Facial Expression
  • Posture
  • Gesture
  • Body Language
  • Touch
  • Eye Contact
